Infinite Monkey Theorem

by Osha

The Infinite Monkey Theorem is a fascinating concept in probability theory that captures my imagination. It states that if I were to provide a monkey with a typewriter keyboard and an infinite amount of time, this monkey would almost surely type any given text, including the complete works of William Shakespeare.

This thought experiment explores the idea that even though the probability of such an event is astronomically low, given unlimited time, it would eventually happen. In this context, "almost surely" is a mathematical term indicating an event that happens with a probability of 1.
