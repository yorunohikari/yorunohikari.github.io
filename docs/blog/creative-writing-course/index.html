Creative Writing Course Project

by Osha

During the last semester, I enrolled in a captivating Creative Writing course. The class proved to be engaging, with our teacher assigning us stimulating projects. These comprised four distinct assignments: crafting a short story, composing five poems, creating a Travel Notes piece, and scripting a drama. Through these endeavors, I gleaned invaluable insights.

Short Story: "Tuhan Tidak Lagi Bersamamu"

In my short story, I delved into the narrative of a fallen angel who, while observing human history unfold, grapples with existential dilemmas. Themes of morality, the nature of suffering, and the essence of existence were intricately woven into the storyline. READ MORE.

Poems: A Selection of Five Poems

My poetic endeavors ranged from contemplations on existence to passionate declarations of love. I even experimented with meta-poetry, leveraging HTML capabilities to enhance the creative expression. READ MORE.

Travel Notes: "Jalan Jalan Malang

For the Travel Notes assignment, we were tasked with embarking on a leisurely stroll through the streets of Malang city, keenly observing our surroundings to later encapsulate our experiences in writing. READ MORE.

Drama Script: "Pada Suatu Ketika

In the realm of drama scripting, I embraced full creative liberty by blending elements of existing Indonesian legends with original narrative concepts, resulting in a captivating new storyline. READ MORE.

Overall, I found immense enjoyment and enrichment in this course, deepening my appreciation for the art of creative expression.
