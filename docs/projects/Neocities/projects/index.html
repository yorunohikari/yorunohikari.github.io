01
Mardial - Leave This Town (Fanmade Lyric Video)
Personal Favorite
Possibly the first lyric video I ever made. I created it during the pandemic. I was in love with Mardial's song, and it felt natural to make some kind of tribute. The art direction for this project was focused on capturing the dystopian / cyberpunk / digital vibe of the track. I decided to experiment with neon motion graphics to match that feeling. I downloaded free sci-fi panel textures from cc0textures.com and used them as canvases for the lyrics. The whole project was rendered in Eevee due to my laptop’s limitations.
TECH STACK:
Blender 3D Premiere Pro Photoshop Motion Graphics

03
Bring Me The Horizon - Dig It (Fanmade Lyric Video)
RECENT
I listen to BMTH practically every day, and this particular song touches my soul more than I ever imagined. In this project, I used my own photos from different times to represent fading memories. I edited out other people from all the photos as a manifestation of my dissociation from the rest of the world as time goes by. I used a lot of BlenderKit assets for this, and rendered everything in Cycles. I managed to pull off this heavy render using Google Colab, my 6-year-old office laptop could never handle it. It would’ve taken a hundred years to render the whole thing locally. Even the viewport playback was barely functional. I synced the lyrics by pure magic.
TECH STACK:
Blender 3D Premiere Pro Photoshop Motion Graphics
